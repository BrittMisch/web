Homemade Pillows

A Fun and Unique Way To Make Memories With Children

Why How More

Why should I make pillows? Can't I just buy my kid a stuffed animal?

There are many reasons to make pillows with children of all ages

  • A fun and unique activity a slumber party
  • Group bonding at summer camp
  • Make a pillow with your child who is scared to sleep away from home.

How to Make Pillows in 9 Easy Steps!

  1. Cut two pieces of fabric into equal sized squares
  2. Place one square topside down on a flat surface
  3. About a quarter inch from the edge line three sides with fabric glue.
  4. Place the other fabric piece on top of the glue covered piece. Let dry overnight.
  5. Once dry, allow children to color on pillows with fabric markers.
  6. Fill with pillow stuffing.
  7. Glue open side with fabric glue. Use clothes pins to pinch together the gluey side. Let dry overnight.
  8. Remove clothes pins. If necessary reapply glue to any holes.
  9. Enjoy!

Ways to Make Your Pillow Even More Special

  • Have children decorate or sign each others pillows
  • Decorate pillows with glitter, puffy paint or more
  • Cut pillow fabric into different unique shapes
